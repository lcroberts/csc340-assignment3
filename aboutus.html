Generic Office Chairs

Generic Office Chairs is a company from Greensboro, North Carolina. We are currently a small but passionate group of designers dedicated to making quality, but affordable, office chairs. We want to keep our designs simple in a way that doesn't stand out. We are always open to new desing ideas and wish to continually improve both our products and the variety in our catelog.
